# Stuti-Vani Foundation
# Brand Guidelines

Version: 1.0

---

# Purpose

This document defines the visual identity, communication style, and emotional language of the Stuti-Vani Foundation brand.

The objective is to maintain consistency across the website, social media, reports, presentations, campaigns, and future digital products.

Every design and content decision should reinforce trust, compassion, professionalism, and transparency.

---

# Brand Mission

Empowering lives through education, healthcare, compassion, and community development.

---

# Brand Vision

To become one of India's most trusted and transparent NGOs by leveraging technology, education, and community participation.

# Logo Usage

Use only official logo files.

Maintain clear space around the logo.

Never stretch the logo.

Never rotate the logo.

Never distort proportions.

Never apply shadows.

Never place the logo on low-contrast backgrounds.

Preferred formats

SVG

PNG
